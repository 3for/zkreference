\presection{About this version}  %Message from the editors
\label{sec:prelim:msg-editors}

\revblock[rev:clarify-this-version]{\ref{it:editorial:clarify-this-version}}
	This is the version 0.2 of the ZKProof Community Reference.
	It results from the help of many contributors, as described in the 
\hyperref[sec:prelim:change-log]{version history} and the \hyperref[sec:prelim:acknowledgments]{acknowledgments} sections,
and in the notes of the referenced ZKProof workshops.
	This is still a draft version, serving as a basis for further development.
	Reaching a stable release requires additional substantial contributions and revision, 
for which a continued engagement by contributors is most welcome.
